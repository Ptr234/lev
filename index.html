CONFERENCE OVERVIEW

The November Blessing Conference is an annual seven-day international gathering organized by Christ's Heart Ministries International. This premier spiritual event attracts ministers, believers, and seekers from across the globe, featuring powerful worship, transformative teaching, prophetic ministry, and vibrant fellowship.
